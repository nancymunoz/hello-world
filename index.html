Princess Cruise to Alaska

Departing from Vancouver, Canada, this 7-day cruise takes you to the breathtaking coastlines of Alaska. Enjoy the stunning scenery, abundant wildlife, and charming ports of call along the way.

  • Day 1: Vancouver, Canada (Departure)
  • Day 2: At Sea
  • Day 3: Juneau, Alaska
  • Day 4: Skagway, Alaska
  • Day 5: Glacier Bay National Park, Alaska (Scenic Cruising)
  • Day 6: Ketchikan, Alaska
  • Day 7: Vancouver, Canada (Return)
